The Fascinating World of Cats

Overview

Cats, also known as domestic cats, are small, carnivorous mammals that are valued by humans for companionship and their ability to hunt vermin. As one of the oldest pet species, they were likely first domesticated in the Near East around 7500 BC.

Behavior

Cats are known for their agility and stealth. They can exhibit varying behaviors from being very social and affectionate to more independent. Cats typically communicate by meowing, purring, and additional vocalizations. They are also known for their sleep habits, often sleeping up to 16 hours a day.
